ABOUT US

CICADA Bank is India's largest private sector bank with total consolidated assets of Rs. 11,242.81 billion (US$ 172.5 billion) at March 31, 2018 and profit after tax of Rs. 67.77 billion (US$ 1.0 billion) for the year ended March 31, 2018. CICADA Bank currently has a network of 4,867 Branches and 14,367 ATMs across India.

 
History
CICADA Bank was originally promoted in 1994 by CICADA Limited, an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
