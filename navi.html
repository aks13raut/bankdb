Kanaz Tarachand | Owner

Kanaz Tarachand started her English Speaking Classes in 1993. She started her classes after graduating and completing a course in Public Speaking from the Indo-American Society and Personality Development from British Institutes in 2002. She has also done a short course with Pramod palekar.Currently her institution teaches housewives, businessmen, professionals and students from all levels of society. With sheer hard-work and dedication, she has brought up her class as one of the best in Mumbai. She has an experience of teaching over 50,000 students. The English Speaking class teaches students how to speak and write confidently. She also trains them in Personality Development.
